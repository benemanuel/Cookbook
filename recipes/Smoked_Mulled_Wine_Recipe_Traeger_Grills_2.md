# Re: Smoked Mulled Wine Recipe | Traeger Grills

## Ingredients

#### main

  + 2 Bottle red wine
  + 1/2 Cup whiskey
  + 1/2 Cup white rum
  + 1/2 Cup honey
  + 1 Stick cinnamon
  + 2 pods star anise
  + 4 whole cloves
  + 1 (3 in) orange peel

## Instructions

1. When ready to cook, set Traeger temperature to 180℉ and preheat with the lid closed for 15 minutes. For optimal flavor, use Super Smoke if available.
2. In a shallow baking dish, combine wine, whiskey, rum, honey, cinnamon stick, star anise, cloves, and orange peel. Stir well until combined.
3. Place the dish directly on the grill grate and smoke for one hour until the mixture is warm.
4. Remove from grill and ladle into mugs leaving the mulling spices behind. Garnish with fresh cinnamon sticks, anise, orange zest, or a combination. Enjoy!

# Sweet Potato Sourdough Cinnamon Rolls

## Ingredients

## Instructions

**Sweet Potato Sourdough Cinnamon Rolls**

## **Dough**

* 350 g sweet potato (blended with milk)

* 250 g tangzhong (50 g flour \+ 250 g milk)

* 2 eggs

* 360 g stiff sourdough starter

* 950 g flour (bread)

* 225 g sugar

* 115 g soft butter

* 9 g salt

* Milk 80–120 ml — begin with 80

## **Filling**

* 345 g butter

* 270 g demerara sugar

* 3 tsp cinnamon

* 2 tsp nutmeg

* 2 tsp ginger

* 2 tsp cloves

## **Frosting**

* 230 g cream cheese

* 230 g mascarpone

* 30 g butter

* 60 g powdered sugar

* 1 tsp vanilla

---


# **Method**

1. Make tangzhong (50 g flour \+ 250 g milk to 65°C).

2. Blend sweet potato with milk.

3. Mix tangzhong, sweet potato, flour, sugar, eggs → 30 min autolyse.

4. Add stiff starter \+ milk (After adding starter, add more only if dough is dry)→ rest 30 min.

5. Add salt \+ butter → knead 5–10 min.

6. Fold 3× every 40 min. Bulk \~4 hrs.

7. Refrigerate overnight.

8. Roll to 0\.5 cm, spread filling, roll up, cut 5 cm pieces.

9. Proof 3–5 hrs.

10. Bake 175°C for 25–30 min.

11. Frost warm.

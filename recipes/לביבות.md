## Instructions

Shred the potatoes. Dry them off and put them in the fridge over\-night. Dry them again. Get that pan hot (water drops should crackle) and then put little oil in the bottom. Drop a small pile of potatoes in the pan and let them sit for a couple of minutes undisturbed. Flip them, season with salt and a little black pepper and then put a weight on them. Two more minutes. Enjoy.

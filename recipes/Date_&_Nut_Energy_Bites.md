# Date & Nut Energy Bites

## Ingredients

## Instructions

Here’s your **full recipe** for nutrient\-dense, naturally sweet **Date \& Nut Energy Bites** — ideal with a 4 PM coffee. It includes step\-by\-step instructions and optional upgrades for flavor and nutrition.

---


## 🍫 **Date \& Nut Energy Bites** (Makes \~30 small balls)

### 🛒 **Ingredients**

* 500g seedless dates (Medjool or vacuum\-packed are best)

* 100g raw or lightly roasted **pistachios**

* 100g raw or lightly roasted **cashew nuts**

* 100g raw or lightly roasted **almonds**

* 4 tablespoons **ghee** (or coconut oil for vegan option)

### ✨ Optional Add\-Ins (highly recommended)

* 1 tablespoon **chia seeds** (fiber, omega\-3\)

* ½ teaspoon **ground cardamom** or **cinnamon** (for digestion \+ warmth)

* 1 teaspoon **vanilla extract**

* 2 tablespoons **cacao nibs** (for crunch \+ antioxidants)

* 1–2 tablespoons **unsweetened shredded coconut** (optional garnish)

---


## 🍳 Instructions

### 🔪 Step 1: Prep the Nuts

1. **Chop** the pistachios, cashews, and almonds **coarsely** (or pulse in a food processor a few times).

2. **Optional**: Lightly **toast** the nuts in a dry pan over medium heat for 3–5 minutes, stirring constantly, until just fragrant. Let cool.

### 🍯 Step 2: Soften the Dates

If your dates are dry:

* Soak them in warm water for **5–10 minutes**, then drain well.

### 🍮 Step 3: Make the Date Paste

1. Add the softened dates and ghee to a food processor.

2. Blend until you get a **thick, sticky paste**. Scrape down the sides as needed.

3. Add vanilla or spices here if using.

### 🥣 Step 4: Combine

1. Transfer the paste to a mixing bowl.

2. Mix in chopped nuts, chia seeds, and cacao nibs if using.

3. Use your hands or a spatula to **knead everything together**. It should be thick and moldable.

### 🧤 Step 5: Shape

* Roll into **small balls** (about 25–30 pieces depending on size).

* **Optional**: Roll in shredded coconut, cocoa powder, or ground nuts for coating.

---


## 🧊 Storage

* Store in an airtight container in the **fridge for 2–3 weeks**.

* Or **freeze for up to 3 months** — they thaw quickly and retain texture.

---


## ☕ Best Way to Enjoy

* With **black or spiced coffee** around 4 PM.

* Eat **1–2 balls slowly**, chew well. Pair with water if needed to digest the fiber.

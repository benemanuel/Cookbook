# SOFT SOURDOUGH ROLLS WITH TANGZHONG

## Ingredients

## Instructions

## 📦 Yield

* **14\-15 rolls × \~120 g**

---


# 🧾 TOTAL INGREDIENTS

## 🌾 Flour (Total: 840 g)

* 145 g whole wheat
* 160 g כפרי
* 500 g bread flour
* 35 g ap flour


## 💧 Liquids

* 530 g water
* 175 g milk


## 🧪 Levain

* 220 g (100% hydration)


## 🍯 Enrichment

* 45 g butter
* 30 g honey
* 16 g salt

---


# 🔴 STEP 1 — TANGZHONG

Use:

* 35 g flour *(from total flour above)*
* 175 g milk


Cook until thick pudding. Cool completely.

---


# 🟡 STEP 2 — AUTOLYSE

Use:

* Remaining flour (**805 g**)
* **355 g water** *(this is 530 − 175\) \[last attempt used 530\+175\]*
* All tangzhong


Mix until shaggy. Rest 1–4 hours.

---


# 🔵 STEP 3 — FINAL MIX

Add:

* 220 g levain
* 16 g salt
* 30 g honey


Mix until combined and moderately developed.

---


# 🟣 STEP 4 — BULK \+ BUTTER

Add during first fold:

* 45 g butter (in pieces)


Then:

* 2–3 more folds every 30 min
* Bulk until \~40–50% rise

---


# ❄️ STEP 5 — SHAPE \& COLD RETARD

* Divide into **15 × 120 g**
* Shape into balls
* Refrigerate overnight (covered)

---


# 🔥 STEP 6 — BAKE

* 200°C
* 15–18 minutes


Optional:

* Egg wash before baking
* Butter after baking

# Sourdough Cloud Cake (Vanilla–Citrus)

Sourdough Cloud Cake (Vanilla–Citrus)

## Ingredients

• 1 cup (120 g) all\-purpose flour

• ½ cup (100 g) sugar (for yolks)

• ¼ cup (50 g) sugar (for whites)

• 1 cup (240 g) sourdough starter (discard or ripe, 100% hydration)

• 6 large eggs, separated

• ¼ cup (60 g) neutral oil (or melted butter for richer flavor)

• ¼ cup (60 g) milk (or plant\-based milk)

• 1 tsp vanilla extract

• Zest of 1 lemon or orange (optional, gives it “cloud” brightness)

• 1 tsp baking powder

• ½ tsp salt

• ¼ tsp cream of tartar (for whipping egg whites)


⸻

## Instructions

1\. Prep the oven \& pan

• Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).

• Use an ungreased 9” angel food pan or tube pan (helps it rise like a chiffon cake).

2\. Mix the yolk base

• Whisk together egg yolks, sourdough starter, oil, milk, vanilla, zest, and sugar (100 g) until smooth.

• Sift in flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix gently until combined (don’t overmix).

3\. Whip the egg whites

• In a clean bowl, beat egg whites with cream of tartar until foamy.

• Gradually add the other 50 g sugar, and whip until glossy stiff peaks form.

4\. Fold together

• Gently fold egg whites into the yolk batter in 3 additions, using a spatula, to keep as much air as possible.

5\. Bake

• Pour into tube pan.

• Bake 45–55 minutes, until golden and springy to the touch, or until a skewer comes out clean.

6\. Cool upside down

• Immediately invert pan on a bottle or cooling rack so it hangs upside down.

• Let cool completely before loosening the sides with a knife.

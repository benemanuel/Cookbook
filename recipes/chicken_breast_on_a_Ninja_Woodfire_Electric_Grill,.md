# chicken breast on a Ninja Woodfire Electric Grill,

## Ingredients

## Instructions

To make chicken breast on a Ninja Woodfire Electric Grill, follow these steps for juicy and flavorful results:


\#\#\# Ingredients:

\- Chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)

\- Olive oil

\- Salt and pepper (or your preferred seasoning)

\- Optional: Marinade or dry rub of your choice


\#\#\# Instructions:


1\. \*\*Preheat the Grill\*\*: Turn on your Ninja Woodfire Grill and set it to the "Grill" setting. Preheat to about 400°F (205°C).


2\. \*\*Prepare the Chicken\*\*:

\- If the chicken breasts are thick, you can pound them to an even thickness for even cooking.

\- Lightly coat the chicken breasts with olive oil to prevent sticking and add moisture.

\- Season both sides of the chicken with salt, pepper, or your preferred seasoning blend. If you’re using a marinade, let the chicken sit for at least 30 minutes (or overnight in the fridge) for more flavor.


3\. \*\*Grill the Chicken\*\*:

\- Once the grill is preheated, place the chicken breasts on the grill grates.

\- Cook for about 6\-8 minutes on each side. The exact time will depend on the thickness of the chicken. Avoid pressing down on the chicken while it cooks, as this can cause it to lose juices.

\- Use a meat thermometer to ensure the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).


4\. \*\*Rest the Chicken\*\*:

\- Once cooked, remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat, ensuring a juicy result.


5\. \*\*Serve\*\*:

\- Slice and serve the chicken breast with your favorite sides or use it in salads, sandwiches, or other dishes.


\#\#\# Tips:

\- \*\*Woodfire Flavor\*\*: If you want to enhance the woodfire flavor, use wood pellets or chips compatible with your Ninja Woodfire Grill.

\- \*\*Don't Overcook\*\*: Chicken breasts can dry out easily, so keep an eye on the cooking time and use a thermometer for precision.


Enjoy your perfectly grilled chicken breast!

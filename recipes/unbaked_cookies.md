# unbaked cookies

## Ingredients

## Instructions

**Ingredients**

·8 tablespoons butter (1 stick)

·1 1/2 cup granulated sugar

·1/2 cup milk

·4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

·1 teaspoon vanilla extract

·1/8 teaspoon kosher salt

·3 cups old\-fashioned oats

·1/2 cup shredded coconut

·1/2 cup peanut butter

**Instructions**

1\.In a medium saucepan, bring butter, sugar, milk, cocoa powder, vanilla and salt to a boil and cook for 1 minute, stirring continuously,
 then remove from the heat.

2\.Stir in the oatmeal, peanut butter and coconut and let cool for 5 minutes.

3\.Spoon 1\-inch balls onto a parchment paper. Cool for 20 minutes. Store in an airtight container.

# Chewy Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Without Butter

<https://www.unicornsinthekitchen.com/no-sugar-no-butter-chewy-oatmeal-raisin-cookiesLearn>

how to make oatmeal cookies without butter that are chewy and delicious. These healthy oatmeal raisin cookies are naturally sweetened.

PREP TIME10 mins

COOK TIME15 mins

TOTAL TIME25 mins

COURSECookies

CUISINEAmerican

SERVINGS[12](https://www.unicornsinthekitchen.com/no-sugar-no-butter-chewy-oatmeal-raisin-cookies/) cookies

CALORIES100 kcal

## Ingredients

### 1x2x3x

·⅔ cup maple
 syrup (or ½ cup brown sugar \& 2 tbsp maple syrup )

·2 eggs

·3 tbsp. water

·½ tsp vanilla
 extract

·1 ¾ cup oatmeal

·¾ cup all
 purpose flour

·¾ cup raisins

* ½ teaspoon Salt (missing
 in original)

## Instructions

1\.Mix maple syrup, water and egg in a bowl using a wooden spoon.

2\.Add vanilla and mix again.

3\.Add flour and oatmeal. Mix well until everything is combined and the dough starts to get together

4\.Add raisins and mix well.

5\.Let the dough stay in the fridge for one hour.

6\.Preheat the oven to 180C/350F, line baking sheets with parchment paper.

7\.Take the dough out of the fridge, using a spoon or an ice cream scoop, make small balls of cookie dough
 on the baking sheets.

8\.Bake the cookies for 16 minutes until their bottoms are light brown.

9\.Let cool completely before serving.

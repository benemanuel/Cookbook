# Ancient cookies

## Ingredients

## Instructions

🍪 Khorasan Date \& Pistachio Cookies


Prep Time: 15 minutes

Chill Time: 10 minutes

Bake Time: 18 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Yields: 12 cookies


🧈 Ingredients


Dough:

200 g ghee (or unsalted butter), melted and slightly cooled

175 g honey

360 g Khorasan flour (approx. 2½ cups)

1 tsp (6\-8 pods) ground cardamom

¼ tsp salt


Filling:

120 ml date spread (or thick date paste)

90 g unsalted pistachios, finely chopped (reserve 12 whole pistachios for topping)

3 pinches ground cumin


🥣 Instructions


1\. Prepare the Filling:

In a small bowl, combine the date spread, chopped pistachios, and ground cumin. Mix until well incorporated. Set aside.

2\. Make the Dough:

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the melted ghee and honey until smooth.

In a separate bowl, sift the Khorasan flour, ground cardamom, and salt.

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until a soft dough forms.

If the dough feels too soft, refrigerate for 10 minutes to firm up.


3\. Assemble the Cookies:

Preheat your oven to 170°C (340°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Divide the dough into 12 equal portions.

Flatten each portion into a disc, place a teaspoon of the date\-pistachio filling in the center, and fold the dough over to seal, shaping it into a ball.

Place the cookies seam\-side down on the prepared baking sheet.

Gently press a whole pistachio onto the top of each cookie for decoration.


4\. Bake:

Bake in the preheated oven for 16–18 minutes, or until the cookies are lightly golden on the bottom and just set on top.

Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.


🌿 Tips \& Variations


Flavor Enhancements: For an extra layer of flavor, consider adding a teaspoon of orange blossom water or rose water to the dough.


Texture: Khorasan flour gives these cookies a slightly nutty flavor and tender crumb.


Storage: Store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days.

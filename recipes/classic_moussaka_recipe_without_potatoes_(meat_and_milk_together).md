# classic moussaka recipe without potatoes (meat and milk together)

## Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:


500g ground lamb or beef


1 large onion, finely chopped


2\-3 garlic cloves, minced


1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes


2 tbsp tomato paste


½ cup red wine (optional)


1 tsp ground cinnamon


½ tsp ground allspice


½ tsp ground cumin


Salt and pepper to taste


3 tbsp olive oil


1 bay leaf


2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley


For the Vegetables:


2\-3 large eggplants (aubergines), sliced into 1 cm thick rounds


Olive oil for frying or roasting


Salt


For the Béchamel Sauce:


4 tbsp butter


4 tbsp flour


3 cups milk, warmed


2 egg yolks


½ cup grated Parmesan or Kefalotyri cheese


Nutmeg, grated (optional)


Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1\. Prepare the Eggplant:


Salt the eggplant slices and let them sit for about 30 minutes to release excess moisture. Rinse and pat them dry.


Brush with olive oil and either roast in a preheated oven at 200°C (400°F) for 20\-25 minutes until golden, or fry them in olive oil until soft and browned. Set aside.


2\. Make the Meat Sauce:


Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté the onions and garlic until softened.


Add the ground meat and cook until browned.


Stir in the tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, red wine (if using), cinnamon, allspice, cumin, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 20\-30 minutes until the sauce thickens.


Remove from heat and stir in chopped parsley. Discard the bay leaf.


3\. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:


In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for 2\-3 minutes, making a roux.


Gradually add warm milk, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Continue stirring until the sauce thickens.


Remove from heat, then whisk in the egg yolks, cheese, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.


4\. Assemble the Moussaka:


Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).


In a greased baking dish, layer half of the roasted eggplant slices on the bottom.


Spread the meat sauce evenly over the eggplants.


Layer the remaining eggplants on top of the meat sauce.


Pour the béchamel sauce over the top, spreading it evenly.


5\. Bake:


Bake in the preheated oven for 45\-50 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.


Let it cool for about 10\-15 minutes before serving.


Enjoy your classic moussaka!

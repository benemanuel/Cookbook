# Kasha

Kasha with Mushrooms, Onions \& Noodles (Kasha Varnishkes)

## Ingredients

1 cup kasha (buckwheat groats), coated in 1 egg

2 cups boiling liquid (water \+ Osem seasoning or broth)

1 large onion, thinly sliced

200–250 g mushrooms, sliced

100–120 g egg noodles (dry)

Olive oil (or schmaltz)

Salt, to taste

Lots of black pepper

## Instructions

Caramelize onions

Heat oil in a wide pan. Cook onions over medium\-low until deeply golden.

Add mushrooms

Add mushrooms and sauté until their liquid evaporates and they brown. Push to the side.

Toast the kasha

Add egg\-coated kasha to the pan. Stir and toast 1–2 minutes until dry, fragrant, and grains separate.

Cook the kasha

Pour in 2 cups boiling liquid, stir once, cover, and cook on low 10–12 minutes (up to 15\) until absorbed.

Cook noodles

Meanwhile, boil egg noodles until just tender. Drain well.

Rest \& combine

Turn off heat, let kasha rest 5 minutes covered, then gently fold in noodles.

Season \& finish

Add salt as needed, lots of black pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. Toss gently.

Notes

Too dry? Add 1–2 tbsp hot water or broth.

Too wet? Uncover and cook 1–2 minutes.

Richer version: replace olive oil with schmaltz.

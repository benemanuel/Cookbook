# Sourdough sandwich bread

## Ingredients

## Instructions

Recipe \-


Soft Sourdough Sandwich Bread

\- 300 grams starter

\- 650 g warm water

\- 50 g honey

\- 40 g avocado oil

\- 20 g salt

\- 1000 g bread flour


Combine all ingredients. Let rest one

hour.


Four stretch and folds 30 minutes apart.

Flour dough and cover. Place in fridge

overnight (8\-24 hrs). Remove from fridge.

Let rest and come to room temp (about 2

hours).


Prepare two loaf pans. Divide dough in

half. Roll and flatten the first loaf into a

rectangle. Roll into a log. Place into pan,

repeat with other loaf, and score. Cover

with damp towel and rest one hour.


Preheat oven to 205\. Spray tops with

water. Loosely cover loaves with foil.

Bake 35 minutes. Remove foil, turn oven

down to 175 and bake another 15

minutes, until internal temp reaches

93\. Allow to cool.

# Vegan Hu Tieu Recipe

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Hu tieu noodles (dried or fresh) | 500g |
| Carrot | 2 pieces (200g) |
| Daikon radish | 1 piece (200g) |
| Shiitake mushrooms | 100g |
| Straw mushrooms | 100g |
| Tofu | 200g |
| Dried bean curd sheets | 50g |
| Vegan ham (cha chay) | 100g |
| Bean sprouts | 100g |
| Fresh herbs (cilantro, chives) | 50g |
| Seasonings (soy sauce, vegan bouillon, rock sugar, salt, pepper) | To taste |

### Tips
- Choose firm, clean mushrooms; rinse and soak in saltwater
- Soak dried noodles 30 min in cold water
- Substitutes: enoki/king oyster for shiitake; wheat gluten for tofu

## Instructions

**1. Prep:** Cut carrot/daikon into 3-4cm chunks. Soak dried mushrooms 10-15 min, halve. Cube tofu, slice vegan ham. Rinse herbs and bean sprouts.

**2. Broth:** Boil 2.5L water; add carrots, daikon, mushrooms. Simmer low 30-45 min, skim foam. Season: 1 Tbsp veg seasoning, 1 tsp salt, 1 Tbsp rock sugar. Optional: 1 apple or loofah for natural sweetness.

**3. Toppings:** Fry tofu and bean curd sheets golden. Blanch sprouts and chives 20 sec. Lightly fry vegan ham.

**4. Noodles:** Soak 30 min. Blanch 30-40 sec. Rinse cold. Toss with 1 tsp olive oil.

**5. Assemble:** Layer noodles, tofu, bean curd, vegan ham, sprouts, chives. Ladle hot broth. Garnish with fried shallots, cilantro, pepper. Serve with soy sauce and chili sate.

### Tips for Clear Broth
- Skim foam constantly
- Add apple or loofah for natural sweetness
- Don't blanch noodles in broth (clouds it)

## Switchel (Haymaker’s Punch)

**Yield:** 1\.5 liters (about 6 cups)  
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes  
**Chill Time:** 1–2 hours or overnight  
**Storage:** Keeps up to 1 week in the fridge

---


### **Ingredients (Metric):**

* **1\.5 liters** cold water

* **30 ml** raw apple cider vinegar (with “mother”)

* **10–15 grams** fresh ginger, grated *(or 1–2 grams ground ginger)*

* **30 ml** blackstrap molasses *or* pure maple syrup *or* raw honey

* *(Optional)* Juice of **½ lemon or lime** (\~15 ml)

* *(Optional)* **1–2 pinches** sea salt (for electrolytes)

* *(Optional)* Herbs (mint, basil) or spices (cinnamon, turmeric)

---


### **Instructions:**

1. **Ginger Brew**

  * Boil **125 ml** of water.

  * Add grated ginger and simmer for 2–5 minutes.

  * Let cool slightly and strain if desired.

2. **Mix Switchel Base**  
In a large pitcher or bottle, combine:

  * **30 ml** apple cider vinegar

  * **30 ml** molasses, syrup, or honey

  * Optional **15 ml** lemon/lime juice

  * Optional herbs or spices

  * Optional pinch or two of salt

  * Strained ginger tea

3. **Add Cold Water**

  * Add the remaining **1375 ml** cold water.

  * Stir or shake well.

4. **Chill**

  * Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight for full flavor.

5. **Serve**

  * Shake or stir before pouring.

  * Serve cold over ice, or mix with sparkling water if desired.

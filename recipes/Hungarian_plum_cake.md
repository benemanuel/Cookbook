## Ingredients

1 baking sheet

1. For the dough
2. 4 pcs [egg](https://cookpad.com/hu/kereses/toj%C3%A1s) (medium, size "M")
3. 190g [butter](https://cookpad.com/hu/kereses/vaj)
4. 190g sugar \- cane
5. 1 large pinch salt
6. 125g [yogurt](https://cookpad.com/hu/kereses/joghurt) \- plain
7. 12g baking powder
8. 190g spelt [flour](https://cookpad.com/hu/kereses/liszt) \- [whole grain](https://cookpad.com/hu/kereses/teljes%20ki%C5%91rl%C3%A9s%C5%B1) (TBL 300\)
9. 1 teaspoon cinnamon (ground)
10. To the top
11. 350g plum preserve
12. cinnamon (ground)
13. For the grill
14. 100g granulated sugar
15. 30g [walnuts](https://cookpad.com/hu/kereses/di%C3%B3) (coarsely chopped)

## Instructions

80 minutes

1. 1

  Melt the butter slightly, add the sugar, mix, add the egg yolks, yogurt and mix, add the cinnamon, the flour mixed with the sifted baking powder and mix them together.

  [![Plum and Grilled Spelt Cubes Recipe Phase 1 Photo](https://img-global.cpcdn.com/steps/3728cf87d7cfeaed/160x128cq80/szilvas-grillazsos-tonkoly-kocka-recept-lepes1-foto.jpg)](https://cookpad.com/hu/step_attachment/images/3728cf87d7cfeaed?image_region_id=58)

2. 2

  Beat the egg whites until stiff and add them to the batter little by little, folding them in carefully so as not to break them too much.

  [![Plum and Grilled Spelt Cubes Recipe Phase 2 Photo](https://img-global.cpcdn.com/steps/25118a51de5a4f7b/160x128cq80/szilvas-grillazsos-tonkoly-kocka-recept-lepes2-foto.jpg)](https://cookpad.com/hu/step_attachment/images/25118a51de5a4f7b?image_region_id=58)

3. 3

  Pour into a buttered and floured 21x31 cm baking pan, then place the halved plums on top and sprinkle with a little cinnamon.

  [![Plum and Grilled Spelt Cubes Recipe Phase 3 Photo](https://img-global.cpcdn.com/steps/158a95ff517d4e15/160x128cq80/szilvas-grillazsos-tonkoly-kocka-recept-lepes3-foto.jpg)](https://cookpad.com/hu/step_attachment/images/158a95ff517d4e15?image_region_id=58)

4. 4

  Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees C for 40\-45 minutes, until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. It's worth covering the top with foil halfway through, as the top will start to brown, but the inside will still be raw, so it needs that 40\-45 minutes.

  [![Plum and Grilled Spelt Cubes Recipe Phase 4 Photo](https://img-global.cpcdn.com/steps/3a1543d3167efe59/160x128cq80/szilvas-grillazsos-tonkoly-kocka-recept-lepes4-foto.jpg)](https://cookpad.com/hu/step_attachment/images/3a1543d3167efe59?image_region_id=58)

5. 5

  When it's ready, prepare the grillage. Put the sugar in a pot on the fire and wait, stirring constantly, until it melts (it will clump at first). Remove it from the fire and stir if you feel there are still lumps, but it's already three\-quarters runny, so that it's just a nice light brown color, not dark and burnt. When it's all melted, add the walnuts.

  [![Plum and Grilled Spelt Cubes Recipe Phase 5 Photo](https://img-global.cpcdn.com/steps/1c305a5cff1ddc03/160x128cq80/szilvas-grillazsos-tonkoly-kocka-recept-lepes5-foto.jpg)](https://cookpad.com/hu/step_attachment/images/1c305a5cff1ddc03?image_region_id=58)

6. 6

  Then, while it's still hot, we put it on an oiled board and roll it out thinly with an oiled rolling pin, then cut it and sprinkle it on the cake. Well, this is what I'll do next time, after baking I'll brush the cake with thin plum jam and immediately sprinkle the grillage on it, because the little ones sprinkled a little bit of the grillage, they also nibbled the scattered pieces off the plate.

  [![Plum and Grilled Spelt Cubes Recipe Phase 6 Photo](https://img-global.cpcdn.com/steps/e3936eaa33fc7182/160x128cq80/szilvas-grillazsos-tonkoly-kocka-recept-lepes6-foto.jpg)](https://cookpad.com/hu/step_attachment/images/e3936eaa33fc7182?image_region_id=58)

7. 7

  The delicacy is ready! :)

  [![Plum and Grilled Spelt Cubes Recipe Phase 7 Photo](https://img-global.cpcdn.com/steps/5ef46937ba7006ff/160x128cq80/szilvas-grillazsos-tonkoly-kocka-recept-lepes7-foto.jpg)](https://cookpad.com/hu/step_attachment/images/5ef46937ba7006ff?image_region_id=58)

# Ginger big soda

## Ingredients

## Instructions

GINGER SODA RECIPES:

\~Ginger Bug\~

10g Sugar

10g Ginger

250mL Water

Feed ginger bug with extra ginger and sugar daily until bubbly

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

\~Easy Mode\~

1L \[33\.8 oz\] Juice of your choice

100mL \[3\.4 oz\] Ginger Bug

Ferment 2\-4 Days

Refrigerate overnight

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

\~Fanta (Orange Soda)\~

2 Oranges (Zested \& Squeezed)

2 Mandarins (Zested \& Squeezed)

1 Lime (Zested)

100g Sugar

1L \[33\.8 oz\] Water

100mL \[3\.4 oz\] Ginger Bug

Ferment 4\-6 Days

Refrigerate overnight

\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

\~Sprite (Lemon\-Lime Soda)\~

2 Lemons (Zested \& Squeezed)

2 Limes (Zested \& Squeezed)

120g Sugar

1L \[33\.8 oz\] Water

100mL \[3\.4 oz\] Ginger Bug

Ferment 4\-6 Days

Refrigerate overnight

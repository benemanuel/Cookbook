# Chestnut liquor recipe

To make chestnut liquor, you will need the following ingredients:


1 pound of chestnuts

2 cups of sugar

1 liter of neutral spirit (such as vodka or white rum)

## Ingredients

## Instructions

Start by roasting the chestnuts until the outer shell is blackened and the nut inside is tender.

Remove the shells and skins from the chestnuts and chop the nuts into small pieces.

Combine the chestnuts, sugar, and neutral spirit in a large jar or bottle.

Seal the jar or bottle and store it in a cool, dark place for at least 2 weeks.

Shake the mixture every day to help the sugar dissolve.

After 2 weeks, strain the liquid through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove the chestnut solids.

Bottle the chestnut liquor and enjoy it neat or in cocktails.

# Electrolyte Drink

## Ingredients

## Instructions

Electrolyte Drink


✳️ Ingredients (1 serving)


¼ tsp banana peel flour (see notes below)


¼ tsp pink Himalayan salt (or sea salt)


¼ tsp cream of tartar


Juice of ½ lemon or lime


1 tsp apple cider vinegar (with the mother) – optional but beneficial


1 tsp honey or a pinch of stevia – optional, for taste


300–500 ml water (still or lightly sparkling)


\-\-\-


🥄 Directions


1\. In a cup or shaker bottle, add the banana peel flour, salt, and cream of tartar.


2\. Add the lemon juice, vinegar, and honey or stevia.


3\. Pour in the water and whisk or shake vigorously to mix.

(Banana peel flour doesn’t fully dissolve — consider stirring, letting it sit, and stirring again before drinking.)


4\. Drink immediately or chill briefly for better flavor.


\-\-\-


⚠️ Notes:


Banana peel flour is rich in potassium but has a strong, earthy taste — ¼ tsp is enough for mineral benefit without overpowering the flavor.


If it's too gritty for your taste, you can steep the flour in hot water for 10 minutes, strain through a fine cloth, and use that infusion as your drink base.


This is a low\-calorie, high\-mineral tonic — great for hydration, leg cramps, and mineral support during keto or hot weather.

# Nachos

![](/images/nachos.jpg)

## Ingredients

### For 3 Servings

* **350g** ground beef
* **1** onion
* **1** can kidney beans
* **250g** tomato purée
* **1/2** teaspoon salt
* **1** teaspoon pepper
* **1/4** teaspoon cumin
* **1** teaspoon chili powder
* **300g** tortilla chips (salted)
* **3** tomatoes
* **4** tablespoons olive slices
* **1** package shredded cheese (Gouda or Mozzarella)

---

## Preparation

### Preparation

* Finely chop the onions
* Dice the tomatoes (not too small)
* Place kidney beans in a sieve and drain
* Fill a (oven-safe) dish with tortilla chips

### Preparing the Meat Mixture

Brown the ground beef. When the meat starts to turn brown, add the onion. Once the onions are translucent, add the tomato purée and the kidney beans. Season the meat mixture with salt, pepper, cumin, and chili powder and let it sauté for a short while longer.

### Combining Everything

Distribute the meat mixture evenly over the tortilla chips. Then, spread the tomatoes and olive slices on top as well. Finally, sprinkle with cheese.

Place in the oven at 200°C top/bottom heat and leave for about 10 minutes until the cheese has melted beautifully.

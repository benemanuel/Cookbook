Here it is!


\* 400 gr Nutella ( 14\.5 ounces )

\* 40 gr Flour ( 3 tbsp)

\* 10 gr Cocoa Powder (1 tbsp)

\* 1 Whole Egg \+ 1 Egg Yolk

\* 30 gr Hot Milk (2 tbsp)


Mix the Nutella with the eggs until combined. Then mix in the flour and cocoa powder (I sifted them beforehand). Lastly stir in the milk. Pour batter into a baking pan (mine is 20x20 cm) lined with parchment paper and bake for \\\~18 minutes at 160 °C (320 F).


That's it, really easy and fast. Enjoy :)


Notes: No need for a mixer, you can do this with only a spatula. I added 2 whole eggs because I didn't know what to do with the leftover white and added 10 g extra flour to compensate. I also substituted milk for soy milk but din't notice a difference. My oven seems to be too cold so I had to bake them an extra 10 min.

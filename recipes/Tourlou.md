# Tourlou

## Instructions

the year.

#### tourlou briam recipeTourlou Ingredients

Vegetables:

2 potatoes – sliced

1 large sweet potato – sliced

1 large eggplant – diced

1 brown onion – sliced

1 large zucchini – diced thick

2 carrots – sliced

Feta cheese for sprinkling once cooked (optional)

Fresh parsley to garnish

For the sauce:

400g crushed tomato

1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

1/2 tsp dried oregano

Salt and pepper to taste

3/4 cup water

#### Tourlou Method:

Arrange vegetables into a large baking tray.

Combine all the ingredients for the sauce in a bowl and pour it over the vegetables.

Mix all ingredients through thoroughly and cover with a layer of baking paper then tightly with aluminium foil.

Bake at 180C fan forced 40 minutes or until potatoes are cooked.

Uncover and cook for additional 15\-20 minutes to develop colour.

Once ready drizzle extra olive oil over the top, a sprinkling of feta and garnish with parsley.

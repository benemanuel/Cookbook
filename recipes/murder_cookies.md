# murder cookies

## Ingredients

## Instructions

300g brown sugar


190g butter


170g molasses


1 egg


400g plain flour


2 1/2 tsp bicarb


1 1/2 tsp cinnamon (used the wrong measure, original recipe called for 1 tsp)


1 tsp mace


60 ml milk


Oven 175°C fan


I used a dessert spoon to size the cookies \- I got 49 from the mix.


Baked for 10\-11 minutes.

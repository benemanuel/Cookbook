# Sweet Potato Sourdough Cinnamon Rolls — Smaller Batch

## Ingredients

## Instructions

**Dough**

* 120 g sweet potato (blended with 30g milk)
* 105 g tangzhong *(21 g flour \+ 105 g milk)*
* 1 egg *(≈ 50 g — no need to split)*
* **150 g stiff sourdough starter**
* 400 g bread flour
* 80 g sugar
* 48 g soft butter
* 4 g salt
* Milk: **35–50 ml** (start with 35\)

---


## **Filling**

* 145 g butter
* 115 g demerara sugar
* 1¼ tsp cinnamon
* ¾ tsp nutmeg
* ¾ tsp ginger
* ¾ tsp cloves

---


## **Frosting**

* 95 g cream cheese
* 95 g mascarpone
* 12 g butter
* 25 g powdered sugar
* ½ tsp vanilla


### Yield

* \~8–10 medium rolls  
instead of a huge tray

---


# 🧠 Small execution notes

* Keep milk conservative — sweet potato varies a lot
* Dough should be **soft, elastic, slightly tacky**, not loose
* With enriched dough: full gluten development comes *after* butter incorporation

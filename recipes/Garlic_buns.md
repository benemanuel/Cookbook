# Garlic buns

## Ingredients

## Instructions

1\. 1 pkt yeast dissolved in 1/2 cup warm water \+ a little sugar (original recipe uses 2 pkt yeast) \- bloom for 10 mins


2\. 1/2 cup shortening \+ 1/3 cup sugar \+ 2 eggs \- beat lightly (I reduced the sugar from 1/2 cup in original since they're savoury rolls)


3\. Add bloomed yeast to beaten mixture


4\. Mix in 4 rounded cups of plain flour and proof for 1 hour


5\. Add 1 tsp baking powder \+ 1 tsp salt \+ 1 rounded cup of flour (total 5 cups of flour like in the original recipe)


6\. Parmesan cheese


Make herby garlic butter using 3/4 cup soften butter and however much garlic u fancy. I use A LOT. Proceed as you would for cinnamon rolls.

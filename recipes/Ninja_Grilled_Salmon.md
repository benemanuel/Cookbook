# Ninja Grilled Salmon

## Ingredients

## Instructions

<https://girlscangrill.com/ninja-grill-recipes/main-dishes/ninja-grilled-salmon/#wprm-recipe-container-28280> 


* 3 lb salmon filet
* 1/4 cup olive oil

#### Salmon Rub

* 1 tbsp kosher salt
* 1 tbsp brown sugar
* 2 tsp coarse ground black pepper
* 1/2 tsp granulated garlic
* 1/4 tsp ground ginger
* 1/8 tsp ground cayenne pepper
*  
* Instructions

* **Make Rub:** Combine all of the rub ingredients in a small bowl and combine.

* **Season:** Cut the salmon in half so it will fit on the Ninja Grill. Brush both sides with olive oil. Sprinkle both sides with rub.

* **Heat Grill:** Set the Ninja Grill to the grill function. Set the heat to high and the time to 20 minutes.

* **Grill:** Place
 the filets on the grill skin side up. Grill for 5\-6 minutes. Flip.
Grill for 8\-10 minutes to an internal temperature of 62c degrees.
Remove from the grill and serve.

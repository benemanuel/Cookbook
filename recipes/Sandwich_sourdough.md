# Sandwich sourdough

### <https://breadbyelise.com/seeded-sandwich-bread/>

## Ingredients

#### Overnight Sourdough Levain

* 12 g sourdough starter
* 64 g water at 77°F/25°C
* 64 g bread flour

#### Main Dough

* 600 g bread flour
* 100 g whole wheat flour
* 490 g water at 77°F/25°C
* 140 g levain
* 14 g salt
* 40 g maple syrup/honey
* 80 g softened butter / 65 g Olive oil
* One egg \+ equal weight of flour

#### Topping

* mixed seeds, e.g., sesame, pumpkin, chia, poppy, sunflower seeds

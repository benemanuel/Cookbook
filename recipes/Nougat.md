# Nougat

Nougat is a type of confectionery made from sugar, honey, and nuts, and it is often used as a filling for chocolate bars or other sweets. Here is a basic recipe for making nougat at home:

## Ingredients

1 cup granulated sugar

1/2 cup honey

2 egg whites

1 cup nuts (such as almonds, hazelnuts, or pistachios), coarsely chopped

1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

In a small saucepan, combine the sugar and honey. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture has reached a temperature of 240°F on a candy thermometer.


Meanwhile, beat the egg whites in a large mixing bowl until stiff peaks form.


When the sugar mixture has reached the correct temperature, carefully pour it into the egg whites in a thin stream, while continuing to beat the mixture on high speed.


Add the vanilla extract and nuts to the mixture and continue beating until the mixture is thick and glossy.


Spread the mixture evenly into a greased 8x8\-inch baking dish. Let the nougat cool completely before cutting it into squares.


Serve the nougat as is, or use it as a filling for chocolate bars or other sweets. Enjoy!

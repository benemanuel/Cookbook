# Zucchini Cauliflower Summer Gratin

## Ingredients

- 1 large head cauliflower
- Kosher salt
- 1¼ lbs zucchini, coarsely grated
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- ¾ cup chopped onion
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- ½ tsp finely ground black pepper
- 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour (or GF)
- ¼ cup milk
- ⅓ cup heavy cream
- ⅔ cup grated parmesan, divided (or more)
- 1 Tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
- 3 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. **Prep cauliflower:** Core and break into pieces. Pulse in food processor in batches to rice-grain size. ~3½ cups.
2. **Preheat** oven to 375°F. Lightly oil shallow baking dish.
3. **Drain zucchini:** Mix in 1 tsp salt; place in colander over bowl. Squeeze to remove water (~⅔ cup shreds). **Reserve liquid.**
4. **Aromatics:** Heat olive oil in large skillet on medium-high. Add onion + pinch salt; cook 4-5 min.
5. **Vegetables:** Reduce to medium. Add cauliflower, garlic, drained zucchini, ½ tsp salt, pepper. Cook 7-8 min.
6. **Sauce:** Sprinkle flour, stir. Gradually add reserved zucchini water, milk, cream on medium (prevents curdling). Cook 2-3 min.
7. **Finish:** Off heat, stir in ⅓ cup parmesan, rosemary, parsley. Pour into dish. Bake 20 min.
8. **Top:** Add remaining parmesan. Bake 10 more min until golden. Optional broil to brown.

**Serves:** 4

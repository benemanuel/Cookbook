# Grilled Lamb & Fruit Salad with Feta and Mint-Lime Dressing

## Ingredients

For the salad:


2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped


1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved


1 cup cantaloupe, cubed


1 cup mango, cubed


1 banana, cubed


200 g (about 2 grilled chops) lamb chops, chopped (seasoned with salt, pepper, and a touch of cinnamon before grilling)


100 g feta cheese, crumbled


For the dressing:


3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil


1 tbsp fresh lime juice (lemon works too)


1 tsp honey (or date syrup)


1 tsp Dijon mustard


1 tbsp fresh mint, finely chopped


Pinch of chili flakes (or Aleppo pepper)


Salt \& black pepper, to taste


1/4 tsp Cinnamon


\-\-\-

## Instructions

1\. Prepare the lamb: Grill lamb chops with a sprinkle of cinnamon, salt, and pepper until cooked to your liking. Let rest a few minutes, then chop into bite\-size pieces.


2\. Mix the dressing: In a small jar or bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, honey, mustard, mint, chili flakes, salt, and black pepper until smooth and emulsified.


3\. Assemble the salad: In a large bowl or platter, arrange the romaine, cherry tomatoes, cantaloupe, mango, and banana. Top with the grilled lamb and sprinkle over the feta.


4\. Dress \& serve: Drizzle with the mint\-lime dressing just before serving. Toss gently if you like everything mixed, or serve with dressing on the side.


\-\-\-


This salad hits sweet, savory, salty, and fresh all at once. The feta really ties it together.

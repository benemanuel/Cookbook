# cookies

## Ingredients

## Instructions

1 cup white sugar


1 cup brown sugar


1 cup oleo (margarine)


1 cup oil


1 egg


1 tsp vanilla


1 tsp cream of tartar


1 tsp baking soda


1 cup rice crispies


1 cup quick oatmeal


1 tsp salt


1 cup flaked coconut


1 cup chopped nuts


3 1/2 cups flour


Mix in order and drop by the tablespoonful


press down with fingers


bake at 350 degrees for 13 minutes

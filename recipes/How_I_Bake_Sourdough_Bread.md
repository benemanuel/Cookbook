Using a oven with auto start.  


evening at your leisure  
21:00 add your pre\-prepared 55 gr flour \+ 55 ml water to starter jar  
put Dutch Oven in oven to turn on automatically at 06:15 for 1 hour at 220C  
prepare and premix 388 gr flour \+ 8g salt in a bowl, keeping a jar of 257 ml water ready for the morning.  

get up to a hot oven  
07:15 take dough from fridge and banneton make cut and put in into the oven covered  
Make dough mix pre\-prepared flour, water, starter and salt

(have 15 minutes to wake the kids up, make sandwiches)  
07:35 uncover bread in oven  
Quick knead for about a minute

(have 25 minutes to take kids out and get back)  
08:05 take out loaf  
take dough and give it one major stretch and fold before shaping it into a dough ball and placing it back in its bowl.

(ready the leave for work at 08:15\)  

Time can be later if needed and less starter is used.  
13:20 quick pre\-shape and a little rest for 10 minutes  
13:30 shaping for the basket, quick roll in rice flour, I place it in a dusted banneton  
15:00 after dough is expanding and feels springy it gets popped into the fridge  
time to wash and dry the bowl and prepare 55 gr flour \+ keeping a jar of 55 ml water

# Smashed Burger

![](/images/smashed-burger.jpg)


## Ingredients

### For 4 Burgers

* **4** hamburger buns
* **680g** ground beef
* **8** slices of Cheddar cheese
* **1** tomato
* **4** tablespoons clarified butter (Butterschmalz)
* Salt
* **0.2** iceberg lettuce

### For the Sauce

* **120ml** mayonnaise
* **1/2** onion
* **1** teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
* **2** teaspoons chili sauce
* **1** tablespoon mustard

## Preparation

### Preparation

* Divide the ground beef into 8 equal parts (85g each) and form into round balls. Place these in the refrigerator.
* Shred the iceberg lettuce.
* Cut the tomato into 8 thin slices. (*Tip: Slice the tomato with the stem turned to the side.*)
* Chop the onion as finely as possible (preferably grate it).

### Sauce

Mix the onion together with all other ingredients for the sauce.

### Toasting the Buns

Add clarified butter to a pan over medium heat and toast the buns until golden brown.

### Searing the Meat

Then turn up the heat and place the meat balls into the hot pan (without oil or fat) and press them as flat as possible. Season the top side with salt and pepper and add a small dollop of the sauce. Once the bottom side is nicely browned, flip the patty. Shortly before it is finished, top with a slice of cheese. When the bottom side is also finished, stack one patty on top of the other. The cheese "glues" them together, so to speak.

### Assembling the Burger

Spread some sauce on the bottom bun. Then top the bun with lettuce, 2 tomato slices, and the double patty. The bun is more stable and easier to eat if the lettuce and tomatoes are under the meat. Finally, place the "lid" on the burger and you're done.

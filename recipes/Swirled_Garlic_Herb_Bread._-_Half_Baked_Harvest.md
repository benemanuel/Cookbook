# Swirled Garlic Herb Bread. - Half Baked Harvest

## Ingredients

## Instructions

<https://www.halfbakedharvest.com/swirled-garlic-herb-bread/> 


●1 cup lukewarm milk

●2 1/4 tsp instant yeast

●2 tbsp melted butter

●3 eggs (room temp)

●4 cups flour

●1 tsp salt

●2 tbsp sugar


In a large bowl, combine flour,yeast, salt and sugar together. Pour in lukewarm milk, melted butter, eggs and start to combine into flour and form a ball. Knead dough for few minutes until it is smooth. Place ball of dough into a well greased bowl, cover and let rest until doubled in size (approx 1 hr)


In a small bowl, mix 6 tablespoons butter, 3 minced garlic cloves( or more!), 1/4 cup basil chopped finely 1/4 cup chopped finely parsley, 2tbsp fresh oregano finely chopped (or whatever herbs you like!) 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese.


After dough has risen, press down lightly and transfer to a lightly floured counter. Roll it out into a rectangle , just like you would do with cinnamon roll dough(approx 13×19") Spread garlic butter mixture evenly all over. Then starting from the longest edge closest to you, roll it up into a log. Transfer log onto a prepared baking sheet (i.e lightly sprayed parchment or silicone mat) Have your log of dough in a vertical position, and using a very sharp knife cut down the middle leaving 2 inches at top end intact. Take the left strand and cross it (exposing the inside of the strand so you can see that beautiful green mixture) over the right and vice versa. Then curve the top under itself and do the same with the bottom. Almost like you're making an "S"

Cover with a tea towel, let rise another hour. Brush with a beaten egg. Preheat oven to 375 F, bake for 25 to 35 minutes until golden brown.

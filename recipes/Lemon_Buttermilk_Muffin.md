# Lemon Buttermilk Muffin

## Ingredients

## Instructions

🍋 Lemon Buttermilk Muffins (with Greek Yogurt \& Honey)


Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20–25 minutes

Total Time: 30–35 minutes

Course: Dessert

Yield: 12 Muffins

Author: Adapted


\-\-\-


🧾 Ingredients:


200 ml buttermilk


60 g oat flakes


200 g wholemeal flour (e.g., spelt)


2 eggs


2 tsp regular baking powder


80 g whole cane sugar


tsp pure vanilla extract


100 ml canola oil


2 organic lemons (zest and juice)


2–3 tsp full\-fat Greek yogurt (10%)


1 tbsp honey


\-\-\-


🧁 Instructions:


1\. Prep Oven \& Lemons:

Preheat your oven to 180°C (fan/convection). Place paper muffin liners in your tray.

Wash the lemons, grate the zest, then squeeze out the juice and set both aside.


2\. Soak Oats:

In a bowl, mix buttermilk, oat flakes, and lemon zest. Let it sit for about 5–10 minutes to soften.


3\. Mix Wet Ingredients:

Slice the vanilla pod, scrape out the seeds (or use vanilla extract).

Beat the eggs, then stir in the soaked oat mixture, sugar, vanilla, canola oil, and lemon juice.


4\. Combine Dry \& Wet:

In a separate bowl, whisk together the wholemeal flour and baking powder.

Add to the wet mixture and stir until just combined — don’t overmix.


5\. Bake:

Divide the batter evenly between the muffin cups.

Bake on the middle rack for 20–25 minutes, or until golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean.


6\. Cool:

Let the muffins cool in the tray for 5 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a rack.


7\. Make Topping:

In a small bowl, mix Greek yogurt with honey until smooth.

Spoon or pipe a little on top of each muffin before serving.


\-\-\-


✅ Optional:


For extra texture, sprinkle some toasted oat flakes or chopped nuts on the topping.


Add 1–2 tbsp lemon juice to the topping for a more tangy glaze

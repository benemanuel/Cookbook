# Sourdough Cream Filled Doughnuts

## Ingredients

### Stiff Levain
- 38 g active sourdough starter (white, 100% hydration)
- 38 g water
- 76 g bread flour

### Main Dough
- 390 g bread flour or 50/50 bread/AP
- 152 g stiff levain
- 100 g eggs (~2 eggs)
- 128 g whole milk (cold)
- 75 g sugar
- 7 g salt
- 75 g unsalted butter (softened, cubed)

### Vanilla Cream Filling
- 360 g whole milk
- 1 vanilla bean pod or 1 tsp extract
- 60 g sugar
- 23 g corn starch
- 4 egg yolks
- pinch of salt
- 25 g unsalted butter

### Other
- 2 L frying oil
- 200 g sugar for coating

## Instructions

### Day 1: Stiff Levain
Mix starter, water, and flour. Cover and rise at 75-78°F (24-26°C) for 8-10 hours until tripled.

### Day 2: Main Dough
1. Mix flour, levain, eggs, milk, sugar, salt with dough hook until just combined. Autolyse 30 min.
2. Mix 5 minutes more.
3. Slowly add butter cubes. Mix on medium 15-25 min until passes windowpane.
4. Bulk proof 6h at 75-78°F.
5. Folds at 1.5h and 3h.
6. Cold-proof 6-8h.

### Day 2: Vanilla Pastry Cream
1. Whisk yolks, sugar, cornstarch, salt.
2. Halve vanilla bean and scrape.
3. Heat milk + vanilla until almost boiling.
4. Slowly pour over yolks while whisking.
5. Strain back into pot. Heat, whisking, until thick and shiny.
6. Off heat, add butter. Add extract if using.
7. Cover with plastic touching surface. Cool, refrigerate.

### Day 2: Shape & Final Proof
1. Divide into 15 pieces (60g). Shape into rounds. Place on floured parchment squares.
2. Proof in oven with light on, ~78°F, 10-14 hours until doubled.

### Day 3: Fry
1. Heat oil to 180-185°C (356-365°F).
2. Fry ~2 min per side, top down first.
3. Drain on rack with paper towels.
4. Toss in sugar while warm.

### Assembly
Stir cooled pastry cream. Pierce doughnut centers, pipe filling generously.

## Notes
- Serve same day as frying
- Yields 15 doughnuts

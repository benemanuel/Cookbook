# 6 Sourdough Bread Rolls daily

## Ingredients

## Instructions

Using a oven with auto start.  


evening at your leisure  
21:00 add your pre\-prepared **83** gr flour \+ **83** ml water to starter jar (using **165** )  
put Dutch Oven in oven to turn on automatically at 06:15 for 1 hour at 220C  
prepare and premix **582** gr (80% whole wheat, bread flour, etc) flour \+ **12** gr salt in a bowl, keeping a jar of **385** ml water ready for the morning. (keeping tap water overnight helps clear the chlorine)  

get up to a hot oven  
07:15 take dough from fridge and banneton make cut and put in into the oven covered *(20 minutes covered)*  
Make dough mix pre\-prepared flour, water, starter and salt

(have 15 minutes )  
07:35 uncover bread in oven  
Quick knead for about a minute

(have 25 minutes)  
07:55\-08:05 *(20\-30 minutes uncovered)* take out loaf

take dough and do lamination (basically give it one major stretch and fold before) shaping it into a dough ball and placing it back in its bowl.

prepare a jar of **83** ml water.

(ready the leave for work at 08:15\)


Time here isn't exact as it can be changed by later if needed when it's colder or/and less starter is used.  
13:20 quick pre\-shape and divide into 6 rolls give a little rest for 10 minutes

13:30 shaping for the basket, quick roll in rice flour, I place it in a dusted banneton  
15:00 after the dough is expanding and feels springy it gets popped into the fridge  
time to wash and dry the bowl and prepare

**83** gr all purpose flour.

# Mascarpone and Sweet Cream Ice Cream Recipe no-churn ice cream

Mascarpone and Sweet Cream Ice Cream Recipe

## Ingredients

1 cup mascarpone cheese


1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk


1 cup heavy cream (sweet cream)


1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)


A pinch of salt

## Instructions

1\. Whip the cream: In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form.


2\. Mix the mascarpone: In another bowl, whisk together the mascarpone cheese, sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth.


3\. Fold the mixtures: Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture, making sure not to deflate the whipped cream too much.


4\. Freeze: Pour the mixture into a container and cover with plastic wrap. Freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight.


5\. Serve: Once frozen, scoop and enjoy!


Feel free to customize the recipe by adding flavors like chocolate chips, fruit, or even coffee!

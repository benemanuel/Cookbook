# Banana peel

## Ingredients

## Instructions

To make banana peel flour, you will need:  


Ripe banana peels

A dehydrator or an oven

A blender or food processor


Here's how to make banana peel flour:


Wash and dry the banana peels.

Cut the peels into small pieces.

Spread the peel pieces on a dehydrator tray or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

Dehydrate the peels in a dehydrator at 135°F (57°C) for 8\-12 hours or in an oven at the lowest setting for 4\-6 hours, until the peels are dry and crisp.

Grind the dried peels in a blender or food processor until they become a fine powder.

Sift the powder through a fine\-mesh sieve to remove any large pieces.

Store the banana peel flour in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.


You can use banana peel flour in place of some or all of the flour in recipes for bread, cookies, muffins, and other baked goods. It is a good source of dietary fiber, potassium, and other nutrients. Keep in mind that banana peel flour has a distinct flavor, so it may not be suitable for all recipes. Experiment with small amounts and adjust to taste.

# English muffins

## Ingredients

250g bread flour

100g AP flour

50g semolina flour

50g whole wheat flour

50g spelt flour

310g water

100g levain

20g salt

## Instructions

1\. Mix the flour water, levain and salt for 5 min in a mixer or by hand

2\. ferment at RT for 6h

3\. Cold Ferment for 15h\+

4\. Turn the dough onto a floured work surface

5\. Stretch/roll to a 1cm thickness

6\. Line a sheet pan with parchment (for proofing) and top with semolina/corn meal

7\. Use a baking ring (7,5\-10cm) and cut out pieces

8\. Put left over dough back together, roll out and cut out additional muffins

9\. proof for 1h

10\. Heat a cast iron skillet on low\-medium

11\. Dust the cast iron skillet with semolina/corn meal

12\. bake each muffin 3\-4 min per side

# Apple cream pie

## Ingredients

## Instructions

\* 9\-inch unbaked pie crust


\* 5\-6 medium apples, pared and quartered

\* 1 T lemon juice

\* 1 c brown sugar

\* 1/4 c flour

\* 1/2 t cinnamon \[OP uses 1 t\]

\* Pinch salt

\* 1 c cream


Sprinkle lemon juice over apples. Place apples, rounded side up, in pastry\-lined pie pan.


Combine brown sugar, flour, cinnamon and salt. Sprinkle 3/4 c of the mixture over the apples. Pour cream over the apples. Sprinkle with remaining brown sugar mixture.


400° for 10 minutes, then 350° until the apples are tender, 45 to 60 minutes.

# Enchiladas

## Ingredients

### For 4 Servings

* **1** onion
* **1** clove of garlic
* **350g** ground meat
* **150g** corn (canned)
* **400g** kidney beans (canned)
* **2** tomatoes (25% of which for the dip)
* **150ml** salsa sauce
* **4** tortillas

### For the Sauce

* **75g** cheddar
* **100g** sour cream (Schmand)

### For the Dip

* **1** avocado
* **1 tbsp** lemon juice

---

## Preparation

### Filling

* Finely dice the onion and garlic
* Drain the beans and corn (washing them is optional)
* Coarsely chop 75% of the tomatoes, and finely chop the remaining 25%
* Brown the ground meat in oil and add the onions after a short while
* Shortly before the meat is finished, briefly sauté the garlic as well
* Add the salsa sauce, beans, corn, and coarsely chopped tomatoes
* Season heavily (!) with pepper, salt, and chili powder

### Sauce

* Grate the cheese and mix it with the sour cream

### Filling the Tortillas

* Spread 1/3 of the filling across the bottom of a casserole dish
* Fill the tortillas with the remaining filling, roll them up, and cut them in half
* Place the tortillas in the casserole dish
* Distribute dollops of the sauce over the rolled-up tortillas
* Bake in the oven for approx. 30 minutes at 200°C

### Dip

* Mash the avocado flesh in a bowl using a fork
* Add the lemon juice and finely chopped tomatoes and mix
* Season with salt and pepper

# Tiramisù

Here's the full recipe for Milk Street's Tiramisù:

## Ingredients

7 large egg yolks


71 grams (⅓ cup) white sugar


454 grams (1 pound) mascarpone cheese, chilled


1½ cups brewed espresso (or very strong coffee), cooled to room temperature


1 package (about 200 grams) crisp Italian ladyfingers (savoiardi) or Petibers (פתי בר) / Petit Beurre Biscuits


How to use: Quickly dip in coffee (1–2 seconds max), or brush with coffee so they don’t fall apart. They’re thinner and less airy than savoiardi but still work well in layered desserts.


Note: Best for a slightly firmer tiramisù texture.


Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting


Optional: dark chocolate shavings, for garnish

## Instructions

1\. Whip the yolks and sugar

In a stand mixer with the whisk attachment, beat the egg yolks and sugar on high until pale, fluffy, and doubled in volume, about 10 minutes. The mixture should fall in a thick ribbon.


2\. Add mascarpone

Reduce speed to low. Add mascarpone a spoonful at a time, letting it incorporate gently. Scrape the bowl and beat for 10–20 seconds more until smooth—do not overmix.


3\. Dip the ladyfingers

Pour the espresso into a wide bowl. Quickly dip each ladyfinger, one at a time, into the coffee (no soaking!) and place them in a single layer in a 9x13\-inch dish or similar.


4\. Layer and repeat

Once you have a full layer of dipped ladyfingers, spread half the mascarpone mixture over the top and smooth it. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.


5\. Chill

Cover and refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight.


6\. Finish and serve

Just before serving, sift cocoa powder generously over the top. Optionally garnish with chocolate shavings.

# gin & basel smash

## Ingredients

## Instructions

* 45ml of ford’s gin. (It would be good with any other gin but Fords really works well with the ingredients)

* 30 ml lemon juice

* 30 ml simple (1:1\)

* 5\-8 basil leaves

* a couple drops of saline solution

* add all ingredients into shaker, muddle basil, shake with ice and double strain into a glass with a large rock

# West Coast Onions

## Ingredients

## Instructions

West Coast Onions  


1 cup water


1 cup white vinegar


1 tablespoon sugar


2 large onions, sliced


Put these in a jar overnight


Drain


Mix in


1 cup mayo


2 tablespoons sour cream


1 tablespoon celery salt.
